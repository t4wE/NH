About us

The autonomous non-profit organization Northern Horizons was established in May 2024 in the Vologda Oblast.

Our mission and goals are:

  • 1) To comprehensively promote the emergence of new specially protected natural areas,
  • 2) To increase and unlock the potential of existing protected areas by strengthening scientific research, academic cooperation and environmental education,
  • 3) Form the regional ecological framework by creating protected areas, ecological corridors and areas of sustainable nature use of different functional purposes.

If you would like to become part of our team, our partner or adapt our experience on your territory, please contact us, we will be glad to have new contacts!
